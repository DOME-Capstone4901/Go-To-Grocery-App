
Go-To Grocery App, Developed by Team D.O.M.E for Capstone 4901 at the University of North Texas.
Android application geared towards aiding users to shop smarter, reduce food waste, and receive AI-powered reminders on groceries.

The app helps users track grocery items, set expiry reminders, and even makes smart suggestions on how to use or restock products. With integrated AI, it supports personalized tips, meal ideas, and inventory management.

Key Features:
Smart grocery tracking with expiry alerts
AI-powered reminders and suggestions
Shopping list planning and management
User-friendly dashboard for inventory tracking
Cloud-based data synchronization (future phase)

Project Goals:
Help households reduce food waste Simplify grocery organization Use technology to make everyday shopping more efficient
